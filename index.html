About Sanaia

Hi, my name is Sanaia Pierre! I am from Chicago, IL, and a 4th-year Software Engineering major. I also have a minor in Architecture. At Cal Poly, I have been a member of the Black Student Union and National Society of Black Engineers. My 2nd year was my first year as an E-Board member for the BSU. I was the Graphic Arts Designer; Then last year, I was the Communications & Media Director and this year, I'm the Programming Director. I have also been a judge at the VEX Robotics Competition that's held on campus since my first year with the Society of Women Engineers. I was drawn to that because I did robotics with FIRST in high school.

Outside of school, I enjoy hanging out with my friends. My friends at Cal Poly and I enjoy going to beach, walking around downtown, going out to eat, watching movies together, and going out. When I visit my friends from high school, most often my friends in Baltimore, I explore their campus, we go out to eat, and take the train to DC. I am hoping to visit them next year, when I can buy a plane ticket.
